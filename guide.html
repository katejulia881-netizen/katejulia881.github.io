How Pixel Conversions Work (Beginner Friendly)

Pixels (px) are used for screens. For printing, we often need inches or cm.

The conversion depends on DPI (dots per inch).

Simple Formula

inches = pixels ÷ DPI

Example

If your image is 1200px wide and DPI is 300, then: 1200 ÷ 300 = 4 inches
