What are Pixels?

Pixels (px) are tiny dots that make images on screens. The more pixels you have, the sharper the image looks. Designers often need to convert pixels into other units like inches or cm for printing.

Quick Tip

Screen size and DPI (dots per inch) can change the final size when printing. That’s why conversions matter.
